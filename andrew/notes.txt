How to bring up the Beagle Bone in NFS

-- Serial Port --
If you are looking at the beagle bone with the serial IO on the far side of the board from you: Blank, White, Green, Blank, Blank, Black.

sudo picocom -b 115200 /dev/ttyUSB0

-- Boot the board --
Press and hold the boot from SD button, then press reset. Release the button, then quickly go over to your terminal and press spacebar to interrupt standard boot flow.

env default -f -a
saveenv

setenv ipaddr 192.168.0.100
setenv serverip 192.168.0.1

setenv bootargs root=/dev/nfs rw ip=192.168.0.100 console=ttyO0,115200n8 nfsroot=192.168.0.1:/home/andrew/emblinux-projects/nfsroot,nfsvers=3
